Privacy Policy

The short version

Rigobert Says does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It has no ads, no analytics, and no account system. What happens on your device stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. The app stores only the following data locally on your device, never transmitted anywhere:

Your personal high scores Which difficulty mode is unlocked Number of free trial rounds used

This data is saved using your device's standard local storage and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

In-app purchases

Rigobert Says offers optional in-app purchases (unlocking hard modes, tip jar) processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see your payment details. Purchase receipts are verified through the platform's own systems.

For information on how Apple and Google handle payment data, see their respective privacy policies: Apple Privacy Policy · Google Privacy Policy

Third-party services

The app uses no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking libraries. The only network activity is the in-app purchase flow handled by the platform (Apple / Google).

Children

Rigobert Says is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The date below will reflect when it was last revised.
