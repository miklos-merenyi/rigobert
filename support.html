How to play

Watch the color combination flash on the disc, then repeat it exactly — tap one color, two together, or all three at once. You have 5 seconds to make each move before the round ends, so speed and accuracy both count.

Four modes change how the disc behaves: Still keeps it locked in place, Float lets it drift, Spin rotates it under your fingers, and Both combines floating and spinning at once.

Common issues

  • Game Center won't sign in — make sure you're signed into Game Center under Settings → Game Center on your device. The app only prompts for sign-in once; if you skipped it, you can retry from the app's leaderboard screen.
  • Score didn't appear on the leaderboard — scores below a small minimum aren't submitted, and leaderboard posting can be turned off entirely in the app's settings. Check that toggle first.
  • Multi-touch combo not registering — press all colors in the combination at the same time, not in quick succession; the disc only lights up the mixed color when it detects a genuine simultaneous touch.
  • Tip Jar purchase didn't go through — purchases are processed by the App Store; try Settings → your Apple ID → Media & Purchases → "Restore Purchases," or contact us if it still doesn't resolve.
